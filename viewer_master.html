This is an exposure tool to show where vector borne diseases are occuring and where they are likely to spread.

Specifically, where vector borne lyme disease is increasing due to geostatistical correlations between to habitat, climate, case counts, and known locations of the ticks carrying the lyme disease vector Ixodes Scapularis.
